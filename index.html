What is AthletiQ?

AthletiQ is a wearable-powered analytics system that helps coaches and analysts understand players' movement, heart rate, and effort on the field. Designed for football, basketball, hockey, and more.

How It Works

1. Wearable Devices

Each player wears a Bluetooth fitness tracker that records heart rate and movement data.

2. Real-Time Sync

Data is automatically synced to a laptop or tablet during breaks via BLE and stored in the cloud.

3. Neural Analytics

A trained AI model evaluates player intensity, fatigue, and positioning.

4. Insight Dashboard

Coaches access clear dashboards with heatmaps, fatigue indicators, and player metrics.

Who Is It For?

Professional and semi-pro teams, youth academies, sports schools, and performance analysts.
